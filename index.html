About Me

I'm Louis ,a Computer Science student at Uganda Christian University in Mukono, currently in Year 1, Semester 2. My academic foundation spans networking, data structures and algorithms, database design, discrete mathematics, and computer architecture. I don't just study these , I try to implement them in labs, on real Cisco hardware, in Python, and in formal LaTeX reports.

My approach to learning is deliberate and methodical. I believe documentation is as important as the technical work itself every project I complete has a proper writeup, diagram, or report attached to it. I've configured enterprise networks from CLI, implemented data structures from scratch, and presented technical work formally.

Outside academics, I'm disciplined ,I play football interact with friends and family. I have a genuine interest in people working with children, helping where I can, and understanding human behaviour. I'm reserved by nature, but precise and clear when it counts.

I have a 3-year professional certification roadmap already mapped out starting with CompTIA A+ and the Google IT Support Certificate and progressing toward a mid-level industry credential by graduation.
